Enkisoma's Pay Go Labs

are guided by 4 pillars

Accessible

Low- cost, scalable and equitable pay-as-you-go smart labs are transforming how Africa’s schools connect, learn, and thrive in the digital age

Affordable

For only $3 per child per month, Enkisoma labs revolutionize digital education affordability, making quality education accessible to all.

Empowering

Empowering over 200,000 students with 150+ offline STEM learning tools, our smart labs ensure uninterrupted, future-ready education—bridging the digital divide un locking potential anytime, anywhere.

Enlightening

By linking high-performing schools to underserved, remote ones, we scale quality education, drive peer-to-peer learning, and foster shared learning

How It Works

Simple steps to unlock your school’s full digital learning potential.

1

Partnerships & Application

Enkisoma collaborates with Ministries of Education and relevant departments to shortlist schools. A comprehensive needs assessment informs the planning and development of tailored computer lab solutions

2

Lab Setup

Using needs assessment data and student population insights, Enkisoma designs and installs a fully equipped, modern computer lab tailored to each school’s needs.

3

Teacher Training

Teachers & lab staff training - We offer comprehensive online training for relevant teachers and lab staff to ensure effective implementation and long-term sustainability of the project

4

Ongoing Support

We provide end-to-end support including collection of the termly $3 user fee alongside school fees, routine maintenance, technical assistance, and continuous monitoring, evaluation, and reporting—capturing learner experiences and measuring the impact of the labs

About Enkisoma Africa

Transforming African education through innovative digital solutions

Bridging the Digital Divide

Enkisoma Africa is a pioneering educational technology company dedicated to democratizing access to quality digital education across Africa. Since our inception, we have been committed to providing innovative, affordable, and sustainable educational solutions that empower students and educators in underserved communities.

Our Mission

To provide equality of opportunity to every child across Africa for a future of dignity and responsibility through accessible, affordable, and innovative digital education solutions.

Our Vision

A future where every African child has access to world-class digital education, regardless of their geographical location or economic background.
