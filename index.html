Educational Applications

Enkisoma's Pay Go Labs

are guided by 4 pillars

Accessible

Low-cost pay-as-you-go computer labs enable every school across Africa to have access to modern hardware and technology infrastructure.

Affordable

For only $3 per child per month, Enkisoma labs revolutionize digital education affordability, making quality education accessible to all.

Empowering

Our computer labs offer 150+ educational applications that can be accessed offline, ensuring continuous learning regardless of internet connectivity.

Enlightening

Our infrastructure supports peer-to-peer learning with teachers from well-performing schools sharing lessons with remote and less privileged schools.

How It Works

Simple steps to transform your school's digital education capabilities

1

Partnerships & Application

Schools are shortlisted in partnerships with the Ministry of Education and ICT. A needs assessment is carried out. All relevant documents are filled and submitted to Enkisoma.

2

Lab Setup

With received data on facility and student population, we set up a modern computer lab in your school with all necessary hardware.

3

Teacher Training

We provide comprehensive online capacity building classes for liaison teachers in the school to ensure effective program implementation.

4

Ongoing Support

Collection of the $1 monthly fee is done when school fees are paid, ensuring seamless operation and continuous support.
