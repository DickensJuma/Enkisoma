Enkisoma Africa and MoEVT Zanzibar Complete Lab Installation at Dr. Ali Mohammed Shein Secondary School

September 17, 2025 Kijangwani, Zanzibar Communications Team

Enkisoma Africa, in partnership with the Ministry of Education and Vocational Training (MoEVT) Zanzibar, has successfully completed the installation of a state-of-the-art computer laboratory at Dr. Ali Mohammed Shein Secondary School. This milestone represents another step forward in the government's ambitious plan to digitally transform education across Zanzibar's islands.

Key Project Highlights

  • School: Dr. Ali Mohammed Shein Secondary School, Rahaleo Ward, Zanzibar
  • Students Benefiting: 1,832 students across all levels
  • Staff Impact: 78 educators (52 female, 26 male teachers)
  • Technology Deployed: 50 desktop computers with comprehensive offline learning resources
  • Educational Content: 150+ STEM applications and digital learning materials
  • Official Launch: Presided by Omar Ali Yussuf, Director General of ZURA
  • Cost per Student: Just $3 per month for sustainable digital education access
